Our Story

From a small team in Ho Chi Minh City to a trusted partner for firms across Australia and the United States

CommonWorks was founded with a simple idea: that architecture and engineering firms deserve access to world-class technical support — delivered reliably, affordably, and with the same care as an in-house team.

What began as a focused structural drafting service has grown into a comprehensive multi-discipline consultancy, supporting builders, developers and engineers with architecture drafting, structural engineering, civil design, surveying, energy rating and estimation services.

Today, with over 30 team members and 10,000+ projects completed, we continue to operate on the same principles that started it all: quality, integrity, and genuine partnership.

Our Mission

To empower engineering and architecture firms to scale effortlessly

We provide seamless, high-quality technical support that integrates into our clients' workflows — enabling them to take on more projects, meet tighter deadlines, and grow their businesses without the overhead of expanding in-house teams.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted subcontractor for firms across Australia and the US

We aspire to set the benchmark for offshore consulting — where every project is delivered on time, to standard, and under our clients' brand. We envision a future where geography is no barrier to exceptional engineering collaboration.
